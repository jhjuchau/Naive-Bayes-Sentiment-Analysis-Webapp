Why Sentiment Analysis?

The the internet has become the primary form of expression in modern society. Because social media has no primary demographic, analyzing the thoughts and opinions of its users can be of value to organizations such as marketing teams, journalists, and even political campaign coordinators. While tools exist to compile and understand the majority opinion, the process is currently largely manual. This requires individuals to sift through thousands of posts to determine how the public feels on a given topic. What is even more difficult is compiling a comprehensive list of the common points of public praise, as well as what users criticize when they speak negatively on a certain topic. We have attempted to simplify this process to allow users to visualize and understand sentiment on keywords included in tweets.
